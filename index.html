The most common questions

What is hosting?

Game hosting is a service that allows you to run multiplayer online games. Hosting servers provide players with a place to connect and have fun together.

Why do I need hosting?

Game hosting is a service that allows players from all over the world to connect to your server. With hosting, players can play your game no matter where they are.

What games can I host?

Hosting Minecraft Java Edition servers is our specialty, but if you're looking for hosting for another game, we're here to help. Contact us at our Discord server, and we will find a solution that will meet your needs.

How can I manage the server?

To manage the server, simply log in to our administration panel. The panel is available on our panel.

Can I upload my own mods/plugins to the server?

Our hosting is flexible and allows you to install any modification, plug-in or even engine. Thanks to this, you can adjust your server to your needs and preferences.

Where can I get help?

If you need help with our hosting, you can always contact us at our discord server.
